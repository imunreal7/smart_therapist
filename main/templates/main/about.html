About Us

The purpose of this project is to overcome the problem that in a perfect world, we all have easy, affordable access to mental healthcare professionals and be able to properly treat these maladies.

But today we do not have that much time to go to clinic for routine checkup. So, we need something which can look after our mental health in this busy life. And also which consume less time to implement its process.
